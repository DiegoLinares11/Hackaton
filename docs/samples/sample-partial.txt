SolarCook 3000 — Product Brochure

Introducing the SolarCook 3000, the revolutionary cooking solution for
off-grid families! Our innovative solar cooker harnesses the power of the
sun to cook meals without wood, charcoal, or gas — saving money and reducing
emissions.

WHO IS IT FOR?
Perfect for rural households and refugee camps where clean cooking is needed.
Families save hours every week that would otherwise be spent collecting firewood.

HOW DOES IT WORK?
The parabolic reflector concentrates sunlight onto a black cooking pot,
reaching temperatures above 150°C — enough to boil water, cook rice, beans,
stews, and even bake bread. Simply point it at the sun and let it cook!

IMPACT
SolarCook aligns with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, especially those
related to clean energy and climate action. Our mission is to bring sustainable
cooking to the 2.4 billion people who still rely on polluting fuels.

PRICING

DEPLOYMENT

SUPPORT
